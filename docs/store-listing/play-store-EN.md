## Full description (max 4000 chars)

Ledger SR is the simplest way to track current USD/SRD and EUR/SRD exchange
rates, alongside global commodity prices that affect Suriname.

✓ LIVE EXCHANGE RATES
Daily updated rates from CBvS (official) and Finabank (cambio/street).
See the spread between buy and sell at a glance.

✓ QUICK CONVERSION
Simple USD ↔ SRD calculator using the correct street rate, so you know what
you actually get at the cambio.

✓ COMMODITIES WITH TRENDLINE
Track oil, gold, silver, sugar, rice, and more. Every card shows a 30-day
trendline — tap it to see the exact price on a given day.

✓ RATE-CHANGE ALERTS
Set a threshold (e.g. "notify me when USD ≥ 40 SRD") and get a notification
when it's crossed. Works in the background.

✓ BILINGUAL
Nederlands and English. Switch instantly in settings.

✓ PRIVACY-RESPECTING
No account required. No ads. No collection of personal data. All preferences
stay local on your device.

✓ WORKS OFFLINE
Open the app without internet to see the last fetched rates.

Sources: Central Bank of Suriname (CBvS), Finabank, U.S. Federal Reserve (FRED),
metals.dev.
